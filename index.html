ERRROLAB

Errrolab is an art studio pioneering Regressionism*, an artistic movement in this world where human creation has almost vanished due to the rise of AI technology. The deliberate misspelling of "error" in its name declares Errro Lab's commitment to imperfection and inefficiency.

REGRESSIONISM

When did generating images replace painting, and writing prompts replace writing itself? Since when did humans stop creating?

We regress. We return to the era when we fully felt our senses, fully engaged our thoughts, and experienced the world with our entire bodies.

Do not criticize it for being imperfect or inefficient. The souls of regressionism strives for exactly this.

In an age where speed is taken for granted, we return to error-filled slowness and inefficiency.

Let us leisurely savor the joys that arise in the process.

Creative Studio

ERRROLAB’s Creative Studio provides the perfect environment for those who had abandoned creation due to a lack of materials or space. Here, there are no judging eyes, and everyone forms a creative community, positively influencing one another. The studio is equipped with a wide range of tools, art supplies, and electronic components, and additional materials are continually provided upon creators’ requests. This free and warm creative space becomes a source of inspiration and passion.

Unleash your ERROR here to your heart’s content.

Operating Hours: Open daily (except for the third Saturday of each month)

Work Exhibition

ERRROLAB’s Work Exhibition is a space where any creator can freely display their work simply by applying, and it is open to all visitors. In a space that does not hinder interaction between artwork and audience, creators, their works, and visitors are organically connected, influencing one another. During the quarterly themed exhibitions, visitors can also explore official merchandise and hands-on experience booths.

Operating Hours: Tuesday to Sunday, 11:00 AM – 7:00 PM
