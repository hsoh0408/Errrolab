ERROLAB

Errrolab is an art studio pioneering Regressionism*, an artistic movement in this world where human creation has almost vanished due to the rise of AI technology. The deliberate misspelling of "error" in its name declares Errro Lab's commitment to imperfection and inefficiency.

REGRESSIONISM

When did generating images replace painting, and writing prompts replace writing itself? Since when did humans stop creating?

We regress. We return to the era when we fully felt our senses, fully engaged our thoughts, and experienced the world with our entire bodies.

Do not criticize it for being imperfect or inefficient. The souls of regressionism strives for exactly this.

In an age where speed is taken for granted, we return to error-filled slowness and inefficiency.

Let us leisurely savor the joys that arise in the process.
